Certified FC


League Championship Certificate:

Awarded to the club that wins their respective league competition.

Cup Championship Certificate:

Awarded to the club that wins a cup competition, such as the FA Cup, Copa del Rey, or DFB-Pokal.

Promotion Certificate:

Awarded to clubs that are promoted to a higher division within their league system.
